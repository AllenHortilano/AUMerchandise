About AU Marketplace

Our Marketplace

AU Marketplace is a student-led initiative that brings the vibrant community of Arellano University together through a digital platform where students, faculty, and staff can buy, sell, and exchange products and services.

Established in 2023, our marketplace has grown to become an essential part of university life, supporting entrepreneurship and fostering community bonds.

By Students, For Students

Our platform was conceived by a group of enterprising Information Technology students who recognized the need for a centralized marketplace within the university.

Today, AU Marketplace is maintained and developed by a dedicated team of student developers, designers, and business managers who are passionate about creating value for the Arellano community.

Community Impact

AU Marketplace has facilitated thousands of transactions, helped launch student businesses, and created part-time employment opportunities throughout the campus.

We're proud to support sustainability through our secondhand marketplace and to provide a platform for student artists and creators to showcase their talents.

Our Mission

To empower the Arellano University community by providing a secure, accessible, and user-friendly digital marketplace that fosters entrepreneurship, resource sharing, and community building among students, faculty, and staff.

Our Vision

To be the premier student-led digital marketplace in Philippine universities, recognized for innovation, community impact, and creating economic opportunities within the academic environment.
